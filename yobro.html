The Global Entrepreneurship Summit (GES) is an annual corporate inter-collegiate summit hosted by IIT Kharagpur's Entrepreneurship Cell. Since its inception in 2007, GES has provided a unique venue for new-age entrepreneurs, eminent business personalities, venture capitalists, and fledgling entrepreneurs to meet and connect, get insights from seasoned entrepreneurs, and promise to drive entrepreneurship to new heights.

About Us

Entrepreneurship Cell,
IIT Kharagpur

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur, was founded in 2006 as a student-run non-profit organisation with the express purpose of encouraging the spirit of entrepreneurship throughout the country. We operate with a passion for instilling an entrepreneurial culture on the IIT Kharagpur campus by inspiring and guiding start-ups.The Events organized by E-cell, IIT Kharagpur are as follows :
